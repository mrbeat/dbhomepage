Open source music HELLYEAH!

Disneybitch is the first open source band in the world! What? Yes!

If you have either the elektron machinedrum uw or the elektron monomachine, or preferrebly both, you can load up the sysexes for our songs on your own machines and do very extreme remixes, if you'd like.

The sysexes are available at github.
